services

STRUCTURAL STEEL DETAILING

Structural steel detailing services involve the creation of detailed drawings, plans, and specifications for the fabrication and erection of structural steel components used in buildings, bridges, industrial plants, and other infrastructure projects. These services are essential for translating engineering designs into precise instructions for fabrication and construction teams. Additionally, we provide advance bill of materials, customized material lists, and FabTrol reports, ensuring thorough documentation for our clients. Our expertise extends to generating EJE and Kiss files, as well as offering DWG/DXF models and customized CNC files and NC DXF files. With a commitment to technological proficiency, we provide EPM/PowerFab reports and IFC model files. Moreover, our services encompass the practical aspects of the project, including field bolt plans, demonstrating our dedication to a holistic approach. To enhance communication and problem-solving, we also provide 3D sketches for RFIs, ensuring a clear understanding of project requirements throughout the entire process.

MISCELLANEOUS STEEL DETAILING

Miscellaneous steel detailing services involve the creation of detailed drawings, plans, and specifications for various steel structures and components that are not part of the main structural framework of a building or infrastructure project. These services cover a wide range of steel elements beyond the primary structural framework, including

  1. Stair Case detailing
  2. Hand Rail detailing
  3. Fence detailing
  4. Ornamental metal detailing
  5. Ship ladder
  6. Industrial access stairs with railing
  7. Safety gates
  8. Platform guardrails
  9. Bollards
  10. Platform grating/ checkered plate
  11. Dumpster gate

Overall, miscellaneous steel detailing services play a crucial role in ensuring the quality, safety, and functionality of steel structures beyond the primary framework, contributing to the overall success of construction projects.

CONNECTION ENGINEERING

In addition to our structural and miscellaneous steel detailing services, we offer connection engineering expertise. Our team provides PE-signed connection calculations for various structural steel connections, including beam-to-beam, beam-to-column, girts, purlins, as well as miscellaneous steel components like stairs and rails. With our comprehensive approach, we ensure the structural integrity and safety of every connection within your project.
